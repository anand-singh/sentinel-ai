# Initial Concept

/conductor:newTrack "Implement Fraud Case Management Dashboard UI"

## Product Guide

### Target Audience
- **Fraud Analysts:** Primary investigators reviewing specific cases and alerts.
- **Risk Managers:** Overseeing broader fraud trends, metrics, and team performance.
- **System Admins:** Managing user roles, permissions, and system configurations.

### Core Objectives
- **Streamline Triage:** Foster faster identification and prioritization of high-risk cases.
- **Deep Investigation:** Provide detailed tools for analyzing complex fraud patterns and rings.
- **Reporting & Metrics:** High-level dashboards and summaries for executive review.

### Key Features
- **Queue Management:** Assigned, unassigned, and escalated case lists with bulk actions.
- **Transaction History:** Detailed chronological view of flagged and related transactions.
- **Entity Linking:** Visualizing connections between suspicious accounts or entities.

### Platform
- **Web Application:** A desktop-focused web portal optimized for intense data analysis.
